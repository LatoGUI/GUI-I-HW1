Intro

Who doesn't love pancakes? I always see people buy these pancake and waffle mixes that I don't even know the composition of. Nothing like my childhood back in Belarus where my grandma made stacks of fresh fluffy pancakes that were even taller than me! So why don't I help you help yourself by sharing my current recipe. These will feel like you are eating delicious clouds.

Equipment

  • Sifter
  • Spatula
  • Pan(10 inch)
  • Whisk
  • Hand mixer
  • Large mixing bowl

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Egg Yolk 2
White Sugar 3 tbsp
Butter(melted) 4 tbsp
Milk 1 1/2 cups
Flour 2 cups
Baking Powder 2 tsp
Salt 1 tsp
Egg White 5

Instructions

  1. In your bowl, whisk up the egg yolks, sugar and butter until smooth. Then add your milk and whisk some more.
  2. Sift the flour in along with baking powder and salt. Whisk these into the wet ingredients until it is smooth.
  3. In another bowl, whip your egg whites into stiff peaks with your mixer. Then fold them gently into the batter until combined.
  4. In your pan on low medium heat (about 350°F) melt some butter. Add in your batter. Flip when bubbles form on top of your pancake. Then cook for one more minute.
  5. Serve it up however you please.
